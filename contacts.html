Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments through our website, mobile app, or by calling our support line at +91 95562 27777.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through your account dashboard.

Do you offer emergency services?

We provide urgent care during business hours. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 108 or visit the nearest hospital emergency room.

What insurance providers do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers including Aetna, Blue Cross, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact us for specific coverage details.

How do I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through your patient portal. For assistance, contact our medical records department at records@medibook.com.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer telehealth services for many specialties. You can schedule a virtual consultation through our platform.
